What We Do

Our Services

Staff & Corporate Shuttles

Staff Shuttles: Daily transport services for employees between home, office, and other work locations.

Corporate Shuttles: Tailored transportation solutions for businesses (including airport transfers, events, client visits, and inter-office travel).

We connect you with verified shuttle owners who will make sure that employees and the staff travel well and safe from the work place to their point of departure.

  • Staff Transport
  • Corporate Travel
  • Airport Transfers
Tourism & Event Shuttles

Tourism Shuttles: Transport for tourists between airports, hotels, attractions, lodges, and tour destinations.

Event Shuttles: Transport for guests attending weddings, conferences, festivals, sports events, corporate functions, or exhibitions.

Azania Shuttle Brokers acts as the single point of contact, coordinating verified shuttle operators to deliver these services professionally.

  • Tours
  • Events
  • Conferences
Private & Group Travel

Private travel: Exclusive transport for individuals, families, executives, or VIPs.

Group travel: Transport for groups traveling together (friends, schools, churches, companies, tourists).

Azania Shuttle Brokers does not just book rides — it manages the entire journey using vetted shuttle operators.

  • Private Hire
  • Group Travel
  • Flexible Routes
School & Varsity Transport

School transport: Daily learner transport between home and school.

Varsity transport: Student transport between residences, campuses, libraries, and activity centers.

Azania Shuttle Brokers operates as a trusted transport coordinator, ensuring learners and students are moved safely, reliably, and compliantly.

  • Schools
  • Universities
  • Learner Safety
