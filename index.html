About Azania Shuttle Brokers

Azania Shuttle Brokers is a professional transport brokerage company specializing in connecting passengers, organizations, and corporate clients with verified, fully compliant shuttle service operators across South Africa.

We act as a trusted link between clients who require safe, efficient, and reliable shuttle transport, and licensed transport providers who meet our high standards of service and compliance. Our goal is to simplify transport coordination while ensuring quality, affordability, and safety for every trip.

  • Network of licensed shuttle operators
  • Strict safety and compliance standards
  • Centralised booking nationwide
